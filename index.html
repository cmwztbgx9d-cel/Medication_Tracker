Everything your health needs, in one place

From a single daily vitamin to a full prescription regimen and the vitals you track alongside it, Vessalia Health keeps everything organized and on time.

✅

One-tap logging

The Today view shows what's due, what's done, and what's next. Tap once to log a dose — or hit Take All. Skip and snooze just as easily.

📅

Schedules that fit real life

Daily, specific weekdays, every X days or weeks, monthly, cyclic (days on / days off), tapering doses, or as-needed — all fully customizable.

🔔

Smart reminders

Act right from the notification: mark as taken, skip, or snooze without opening the app.

🩺

Daily health check-ins

Log weight, blood pressure, glucose, mood, and pain — or create your own measures — each on its own simple entry page. Star the ones you use most.

📝

Side effects & conditions

Note how a medicine makes you feel, and keep the health conditions you're managing in one place — active or resolved.

📈

Trends & insights

A color-coded adherence calendar, vitals charts, and insights that compare your numbers on the days you take your medication versus the days you don't.

🛡️

Built-in safety checks

Interaction and duplicate-ingredient warnings, plus refill alerts before you run out — all checked offline, on your device.

🗓️

Appointments & doctor report

Keep your next visit close, get a heads-up about refills you'll need before it, and share a clean, doctor-ready report.

🍎

Apple Health & Caregiver Mode

Optional two-way Apple Health sync for weight, blood pressure, and glucose — and manage up to five separate profiles for the people you care for.

Private by design

Your health data is nobody's business but yours.

On your device Everything you enter is stored locally. Vessalia Health has no servers and uploads nothing.
No account No sign-up, no login, no cloud account, no tracking, no ads.
Apple Health, your choice Sync is optional and off until you turn it on — it uses Apple's on-device Health, never our servers.
