Privacy Policy

Vessalia Health · Effective July 10, 2026

← Back to home

The short version: Vessalia Health collects no data. Everything you enter — medications, vitals, and health notes — stays on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no advertising. Apple Health sync is optional and handled entirely by Apple on your device. We cannot see your data, and neither can anyone else.

Who we are

Vessalia Health is developed by Every Storm Industries LLC ("we," "us"). This policy describes how the Vessalia Health iOS app handles your information.

What we collect

Nothing. Vessalia Health does not collect, transmit, store remotely, share, or sell any personal information or usage data — including your medication names, doses, schedules, dose history, health measurements, side effects, conditions, appointments, notes, or any identifiers. The app contains no analytics frameworks, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party code that communicates over the internet.

Where your data lives

All information you enter — medications, schedules, dose logs, supply counts, health check-ins (such as weight, blood pressure, glucose, mood, pain, and any custom measures), side effects, health conditions, appointments, caregiver profiles, notes, and settings — is stored in a local database on your device only. A small summary of today's schedule (medication names, doses, times, and completion status) is also stored in the app's private, sandboxed app group container on your device so the home screen widget can display it. None of this ever leaves your device.

Apple Health (HealthKit)

Vessalia Health can optionally sync a few measures — weight, blood pressure, and blood glucose — with Apple Health. This feature is off until you turn it on and grant permission. When enabled, the app reads those values from and writes them to Apple Health using Apple's HealthKit framework. HealthKit data is stored on your device and is controlled entirely by you in the Apple Health app and your iOS privacy settings. If you use Apple's iCloud Health, Apple — not us — may sync it across your own devices under Apple's terms and end-to-end encryption. Vessalia Health never sends this data to us or any third party, and we never see it. You can turn sync off at any time in Settings, and revoke access in the iPhone Settings app under Privacy & Security → Health.

Notifications

Dose reminders and refill alerts are local notifications, scheduled and delivered entirely by iOS on your device. No notification content is sent to us or to any third party.

Face ID and Touch ID

If you enable the optional app lock, authentication is performed by iOS using its LocalAuthentication framework. The app only receives a yes/no result — it never sees, stores, or has access to your biometric data.

Data export

You can export your data as a CSV or JSON file at any time from Settings → Data. Exports are created on your device and shared only with the apps or people you choose through the iOS share sheet. Once you share an export, its handling is governed by the destination you chose.

Deleting your data

You are in full control. Use Settings → Data → Clear All Data to permanently erase all medications, health data, and history, or simply delete the app — either removes everything, because no copy exists anywhere else. Anything you previously wrote to Apple Health stays in Apple Health; remove it there if you wish.

Children's privacy

Vessalia Health does not collect data from anyone, including children. The app is intended for general audiences.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of the app ever changes how data is handled (for example, adding optional iCloud sync), we will update this policy and note it clearly in the app's release notes before the change takes effect. The current version of this policy will always be available at this page.
